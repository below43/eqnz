EQNZ

NZ Earthquake Post Monitor

Live monitor of #eqnz and geonet posts.

EQNZ.nz was built post Sep 4 #chch quake to monitor #eqnz and geonet tweets. Following Twitter/X disabling their API and a subsequent mass-exodus, this site was updated to aggregate Bluesky posts in 2024.
